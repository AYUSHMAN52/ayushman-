About WhatsApp

More than 3 billion people in over 180 countries use WhatsApp1 to stay in touch with friends and family, anytime and anywhere. WhatsApp is free2 and offers simple, secure, reliable messaging and calling, available on phones all over the world.
1 And yes, the name WhatsApp is a pun on the phrase What's Up.

2 Data charges may apply.

In 2014 WhatsApp was acquired by Facebook (Meta). Today more than 2 billion users use WhatsApp every day.

WhatsApp allows users to send text messages, voice messages, images, videos and documents easily.
